🏏 The Game

Cricket Casino is a 6-ball over betting game. Place your bet before the over starts, then bowl each ball one at a time. Your payout multiplier grows with every run you score — but if you're dismissed (WICKET), you lose your entire bet immediately.

⚡ Ball Outcomes
WICKET You're dismissed — over ends instantly and you lose your full bet. No payout.
DOT No runs scored. A legal ball is used up but the over continues.
1 / 2 / 3 Runs added to your score. Your payout multiplier increases accordingly.
FOUR! Boundary! 4 runs added to your score automatically.
SIX! Maximum! 6 runs added. The crowd goes wild.
WIDE Illegal delivery — +1 run added automatically, then you get a FREE bonus ball with a 2× run multiplier. A wicket on the bonus ball still ends the over at 0×.
NO BALL Illegal delivery — +1 run added automatically, then you get a FREE bonus ball with a 3× run multiplier. A wicket on the bonus ball still ends the over at 0×.
💰 Payouts

Payouts are based on total runs scored when the over ends (all 6 legal balls bowled or you cash out). The payout table in your Scorecard shows the exact multiplier for every run total from 0 to 36. Scoring 36 or more runs pays the maximum of 250×.

📈 Cash Out = Your Expected Value (EV)

The Cash Out value shown is your Expected Value (EV) — the mathematically calculated fair worth of your position right now, based on your current runs and how many balls remain. Cashing out gives you exactly what your bet is statistically worth at that moment. It is neither a profit nor a loss trick — it is the true average outcome if you played this situation thousands of times.
